Nature's Finest

I moved to Tennessee from Southern Illinois on whim with my parents in 2011. I had
no idea that I would fall in love with this state and the surrounding states as well.
It is as if I became a Southerner overnight. The beauty of this stated easily made me an outdoorsy
person. Being that my family and I travel a lot in the South, every now and again, I am able to
cruise the Smoky Mountains and bask in the glory of Nature. These pictures are part of my memory.
